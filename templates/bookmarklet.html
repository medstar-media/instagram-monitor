📊 MedStar Scraper Tool

Drag the button below to your bookmarks bar to scrape Instagram profiles right from your browser.

1

Show your Bookmarks Bar

Press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or ⌘+Shift+B (Mac) to show the bookmarks bar in Chrome.

2

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar

Click and drag the purple button below up to your bookmarks bar:

📊 Scrape to MedStar
💡 Tip: Don't click it here — drag it to your bookmarks bar! Then use it on any Instagram profile page.
3

Use it on any Instagram profile

Go to any Instagram profile page (e.g. instagram.com/garyvee) and click the "📊 Scrape to MedStar" bookmark. It will:

✅ Fetch the profile info and latest 30 posts
✅ Send them to your MedStar dashboard
✅ Auto-add the profile if it's new
✅ Redirect you to the dashboard when done

!

Important Notes

You must be logged into Instagram in the same browser for this to work. The bookmarklet uses your logged-in session to access the data.

If you see an error about "Login required", make sure you're signed into Instagram in that browser tab first.
